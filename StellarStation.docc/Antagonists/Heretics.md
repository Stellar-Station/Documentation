# Heretics

Profane, eldritch, and heretical. Solo antagonists that seek to corrupt reality.

## Basics

Heretics are a roundstart and midround antagonist.

At most 3 roundstart, and cannot spawn with Cybersun Assassins.

Heretics should not work together.

## Objectives

### Guaranteed

- Transcend your flesh (maximize your progression)
- Survive

### Randomized

- Sacrifice `n` players in rituals
- Sacrifice `n` critters in rituals
- Unleash a Heretical Heart in `<location>`
- Curse `n` players directly
- Curse `<specific object>`
